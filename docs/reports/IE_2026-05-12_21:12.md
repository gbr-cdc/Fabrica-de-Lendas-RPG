## Objective
Add full formula breakdowns to the battle history stream so both developers and the View layer can see all elements of an attack and damage resolution.

- Added `HistoryEmitter.dmg_calc(target_id, pda, gda, mda, modifier, final)` → `DMG_CALC|...`

- Emitted `DMG_CALC` after `on_damage_calculation` and before applying damage.
